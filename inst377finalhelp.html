What is AniList?

AniList is a website that users can use to keep track of anime, categorizing them into categories such as completed, watching, rewatching, dropped, and plan to watch. The website also allows users to favorite anime, add their thoughts to them, view other people's anime lists, and post on the AniList forum.

Why am I unable to receive recommendations?

Your AniList profile might be on private. Please set it to public.

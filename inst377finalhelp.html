What is AniList?

AniList is a website that users can use to keep track of anime, categorizing them into sections such as completed, watching, rewatching, dropped, and plan to watch. The website also allows users to favorite anime, add their thoughts to them, view other people's anime lists, and post on the AniList forum.

Why am I unable to receive recommendations?

Your AniList profile might be set to private. Please make sure your profile is set to public to enable the recommendation feature. To make your AniList profile visible, on the AniList website, go to settings, account, then scroll down to privacy. Select either public or semi-public.
